Danger Tone Speaker

2026

Branding and Packaging

Danger Tone is a bluetooth speaker for fighter pilots. I challenged myself to come up with a niche target market and create a product, brand, packaging for that audience. This bluetooth speaker has suction cups, the Top Gun soundtrack and a sonic boom volume option. The branding is loud, nostalgic, and stands out against the boring competition. For this project I designed all four sides of the logo, speaker packaging, a billboard, a mobile and computer website, business cards, and patches.
