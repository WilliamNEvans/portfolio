About

I’m a graphic designer located in San Francisco, California. I’m studying design at the University of San Francisco and plan to stay in the city after graduation. My goals in design and art are to create thought-provoking work that challenges people’s emotions. Some of my focuses are painting, web design, product design, art history, video editing and illustration. I am inspired by the work of David Carson, Francis Bacon, Otto Dix, and Fred Woodward.
